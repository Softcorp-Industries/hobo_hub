Renfrew Recovery Center

1611 Remington Rd NE | Calgary, AB | T2E 5K6

Phone: (403) 297-3337


Provides a structured environment to support people withdrawing from alcohol and other drugs. There is also crisis stabilization for people who gamble.

A 24 / 7 service that helps people detoxifying from alcohol and other drugs in a safe, controlled setting.

Services:

  • stabilizing health

  • assessment

  • information sessions

  • introduction to self-help groups

  • addiction treatment planning

  • discharge planning

  • referral to other programs/services


ADMISSIONS ONLY BETWEEN: 7:00 AM to 8:00 AM daily. Clients are advised to arrive between 7:00 AM - 7:15 AM to have their name taken by security for admission consideration. Entry into the building will not be granted prior to 7:00 AM. Clients should be especially mindful of this during inclement weather days, if planning on arriving to the site prior to 7:00 AM.
